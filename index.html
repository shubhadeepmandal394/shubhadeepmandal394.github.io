Vehicle Authentication Technology

This Technology helps the Security Personnel of any residential societies, business complex, parking spaces, or any real estate buildings in the detection, identification, and monitoring of vehicles entering/leaving the premises by using image processing of the license plate numbers of the vehicles using our android application and matching the same with the database.

The idea is a simple website that is used by the authorities to register residents' names, addresses, phone numbers, vehicle models, and license plate numbers which are then saved in the server database. The website is also used for registering a new vehicle in the residential complex. An Android app used in the entry point to scan the number plates of the cars entering the premises and these scanned numbers are matched with the database. A successful match signifies the vehicle is known and belongs to the complex. On the other hand, an unsuccessful match will signify the car doesn't belong to anyone in the complex.
